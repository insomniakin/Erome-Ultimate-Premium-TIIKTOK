✨ Features

📱 TikTok-Style Feed

Phone-shaped vertical scroll with background album preloading. When you're near the end, the next album is already loaded — just like TikTok loading the next person.

💾 Downloads

One-click single download, bulk ZIP packaging (JSZip), smart filenames, background queue with progress tracking, M3U8/HLS auto-detection.

🎬 Video Enhancements

Speed control (0.5× to 3×), flip horizontal, Picture-in-Picture, Cinema Mode, instant download — all as Video.js control buttons.

📊 Smart Sorting

Sort by views, by video count (most videos first), or by photo count. Toggle direction instantly. Video-only filter hides photo-only albums.

❤️ Erome-Native Buttons

TikTok feed includes every Erome action: Like, Favorite, Comment, Share, Save, Copy, Report — with real like/favorite counts from the album page.

🔞 NSFW & Privacy

One-click blur toggle, hide photos/videos independently, redirect/popup blocker stops aggressive redirects.

🎨 Modern UI

Twitter-style popup cards, dark theme with purple accent, toast notifications, animated splash screen with the Erome logo.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Space=pause, L=like, D=download, ↑↓=scroll, Esc=close. Works in TikTok mode and on regular video pages.
